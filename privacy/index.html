Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [TO BE SET ON LAUNCH]

Bennett Veterinary Service (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect from visitors to bennettvet.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact form submissions. When you use our contact form, we receive the name, email address, phone number (if you choose to provide it), and message you send. This information is delivered to Dr. Bennett’s email inbox via Formspree, our form-handling service.
  • Server access logs. Our hosting provider (Netlify) automatically logs basic technical information about each visit — IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and date/time. These logs are used only to maintain the site and identify abuse.
  • Cookies. This website does not set tracking cookies for advertising or analytics. Netlify and Formspree may use minimal session cookies as a normal part of providing their services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit only to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your information to anyone, ever.

How Long We Keep Your Information

Form submissions are kept in Dr. Bennett’s email indefinitely so we can refer back to past communications about your pet. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Pet Medical Records

Veterinary medical records about your pet are kept confidential and are shared only with you, your designated alternate veterinarian, or as required by law (for example, in a public health investigation).

Third Parties

  • Formspree processes our contact form submissions. See Formspree’s privacy policy.
  • Netlify hosts our website. See Netlify’s privacy policy.

Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we have collected about you.
  • Request deletion of that personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt out of sale or sharing of personal information.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

We do not sell or share your personal information with any third party for monetary or other valuable consideration, ever. The only third parties who receive information you submit are the service providers listed above (Formspree, Netlify), which use the data only to operate this website and the contact form on our behalf.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at the address below or call (925) 519-2316. We will respond within 45 days as required by California law.

Children

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” at the top will be updated to reflect any changes.
