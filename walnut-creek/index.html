Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Walnut Creek

Walnut Creek is home for Dr. Bennett — born and raised in the city, he has been caring for Walnut Creek pets and their families for many years. Whether you live near downtown’s Broadway Plaza, along the Iron Horse Trail, or in the established neighborhoods looking up at Mt. Diablo, Dr. Bennett can come to you.

When the time comes to say goodbye to a beloved pet, in-home euthanasia is a gentler alternative to a clinic visit. Your pet stays calm in their familiar surroundings, you stay close throughout, and there is time — as much as you need — for goodbye.

The Visit, Step by Step

Visits are shaped to your family, not to a schedule. Dr. Bennett begins by sitting with you and your pet. Sedation comes first; the final step comes only after your pet is completely relaxed. The full process — including aftercare — is described on the in-home pet euthanasia page.
