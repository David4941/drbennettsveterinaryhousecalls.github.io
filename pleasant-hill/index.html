Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Pleasant Hill

Pleasant Hill’s tree-lined streets, family neighborhoods, and easy view of Mt. Diablo make it home to many devoted pet owners. From the homes near Diablo Valley College to the established neighborhoods along Contra Costa Boulevard, Dr. Bennett brings expert house-call care — including peaceful in-home euthanasia when the time comes.

In-home euthanasia is the gentler option — your pet stays in their familiar surroundings, you stay close, and there is time, as much as you need, for goodbye.

How Dr. Bennett Approaches a Visit

Each visit begins with conversation, never instructions. Your pet receives gentle sedation; the final step comes only once they are at peace. You stay with them as long as you wish. The in-home pet euthanasia page describes the full process in detail.

Pleasant Hill Wellness and Medical Care

Routine veterinary care also comes to your door in Pleasant Hill: wellness exams, vaccinations, skin and ear consults, chronic disease management, and hospice. The services page has the full list.
