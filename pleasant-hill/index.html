House-Call Veterinary Care in Pleasant Hill

From the Pleasant Hill War Memorial area, Gregory Gardens, and the established neighborhoods near Diablo Valley College, Dr. Bennett brings compassionate house-call veterinary care to Pleasant Hill families and their pets. No carriers, no waiting rooms, no unfamiliar smells — just calm, unhurried attention for your pet in the place they feel safest.

  • Wellness exams and vaccinations
  • Veterinary dermatology and skin care
  • Illness diagnostics and treatment
  • Chronic disease management — diabetes, thyroid, kidney
  • Hospice and comfort care
  • Health certificates

Why a House Call?

Many pets find clinic visits stressful — the car ride, the carrier, the unfamiliar smells, the other animals. For Pleasant Hill families who would rather their pet stay calm and comfortable, a house call means Dr. Bennett comes to you. There is no waiting room, no rush, and you stay with your pet through the entire visit.

End-of-Life Care

For families facing the decision of saying goodbye to a beloved pet, Dr. Bennett offers compassionate in-home euthanasia through Pets for Angels — a separate practice dedicated exclusively to peaceful, dignified end-of-life care at home.
