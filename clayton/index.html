Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Clayton

Clayton sits at the foot of Mt. Diablo, where small-town quiet meets stunning natural beauty. From the homes near the historic downtown to the foothill neighborhoods just below the state park, Dr. Bennett brings expert house-call veterinary care to Clayton families and their pets.

Choosing in-home care means your pet remains where they feel safest, you remain close, and the time for goodbye is yours, not a clinic's.

How the Process Works

Dr. Bennett works at your pace. There are no waiting rooms or schedules. Your pet receives gentle sedation first, and the final step comes only after they are completely at peace. For the full walk-through — sedation, what happens, aftercare options — see our in-home pet euthanasia page.
