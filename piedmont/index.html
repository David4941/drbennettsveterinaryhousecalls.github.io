Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Piedmont

Piedmont’s tree-lined streets, quiet residential character, and beloved Piedmont Park make it home to many devoted pet owners. From the mid-century homes near the city center to the families along Highland Avenue and through the neighborhoods bordering the Oakland Hills, Dr. Bennett brings expert house-call care — including peaceful in-home euthanasia when the time comes.

The choice to euthanize at home means no carrier, no clinic anxiety, no waiting room — just your pet, your family, and the time you need to say goodbye.

What a Home Visit Looks Like

Visits are gentle and unhurried. Dr. Bennett sits with you and your pet first, listens, and only proceeds when you are ready. The in-home pet euthanasia page walks through the full process — sedation, the final step, and the time afterward.

Routine Care for Piedmont Pets
