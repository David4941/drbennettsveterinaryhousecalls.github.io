Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Moraga

Moraga’s quiet, oak-shaded streets and connection to St. Mary’s College give the town a relaxed, neighborly feel. Whether you live in Rheem Valley, near Campolindo, or up along Moraga Way, Dr. Bennett brings calm, expert in-home veterinary care to Moraga — including dignified end-of-life support when the time comes.

In-home euthanasia is the gentler option — your pet stays in their familiar surroundings, you stay close, and there is time, as much as you need, for goodbye.

How Dr. Bennett Approaches a Visit

Each visit begins with conversation, never instructions. Your pet receives gentle sedation; the final step comes only once they are at peace. You stay with them as long as you wish. The in-home pet euthanasia page describes the full process in detail.

Moraga Wellness and Medical Care

Routine veterinary care also comes to your door in Moraga: wellness exams, vaccinations, skin and ear consults, chronic disease management, and hospice. The services page has the full list.
