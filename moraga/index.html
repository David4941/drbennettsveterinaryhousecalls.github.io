Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Moraga

Moraga’s quiet, oak-shaded streets and connection to St. Mary’s College give the town a relaxed, neighborly feel. Whether you live in Rheem Valley, near Campolindo, or up along Moraga Way, Dr. Bennett brings calm, expert in-home veterinary care to Moraga — including dignified end-of-life support when the time comes.

For families facing the difficult decision to say goodbye to a beloved pet, in-home euthanasia keeps your pet calm in familiar surroundings, allows you to stay close throughout, and gives you all the time you need.

What to Expect

Dr. Bennett begins each visit with a quiet conversation. There is no rush. Your pet receives gentle sedation first, drifting peacefully to sleep before anything else. Once your pet is fully relaxed, the final step is quick and painless. Afterward, you may stay with your pet as long as you wish.

For a complete description of the process, please see our in-home pet euthanasia page.
