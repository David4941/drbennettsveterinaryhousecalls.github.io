Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: [TO BE SET ON LAUNCH]

Bennett Veterinary Service is committed to ensuring that this website is accessible to people with disabilities. We believe everyone — visitors and pet families alike — should be able to find information about our services and reach us easily.

Standards We Work Toward

We aim for this site to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, the international standard for web accessibility.

What We Have Built In

  • Keyboard navigation. Every page can be navigated using the Tab key alone. A “Skip to main content” link is the first focusable item on each page.
  • Screen-reader support. All images include descriptive alternative text. Headings follow a logical order (H1 → H2 → H3). Forms use clearly associated labels.
  • Semantic structure. Pages use proper HTML landmarks (header, navigation, main, footer) so assistive technology can move between regions efficiently.
  • Readable typography. Body text is set at a size that scales with browser zoom; line length is limited for comfortable reading.
  • Color contrast. Text colors meet WCAG AA contrast requirements against their backgrounds.
  • Responsive design. Layout adapts to phone, tablet, and desktop screens without horizontal scrolling.
  • No motion or auto-play. Nothing flashes, auto-rotates, or plays sound without your action.

Known Limitations

This site is built and maintained by a small practice. If you encounter any accessibility issue — broken keyboard focus, unreadable text, an image without proper alt text, or anything else — we want to know so we can fix it.

Telling Us About a Problem

The fastest way to reach us about an accessibility issue is by phone: (925) 519-2316. You may also use our contact form or email [email TBD]. Please describe the page you were on, what you were trying to do, and what went wrong. We aim to respond within one business day and to fix verified issues promptly.

In-Person Visits

As a house-call practice, Dr. Bennett comes to you. If you have specific access needs — mobility, language, or anything else — please let us know when you call. We are glad to adapt how a visit unfolds so it works for your family.

Updates

This statement may be updated as the website evolves. The “Effective Date” above will be revised when changes are made.
