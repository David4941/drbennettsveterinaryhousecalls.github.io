Compassionate Veterinary Care, at Home in the Oakland Hills

From Montclair’s village shops to the wooded hillside homes above, the Oakland Hills are a beloved community with deep roots and a strong sense of place. Whether you live in Montclair, Crocker Highlands, or one of the upper Piedmont-adjacent neighborhoods, Dr. Bennett brings calm, expert veterinary care to Oakland Hills families and their pets — right at home.

No carrier, no clinic anxiety, no waiting room — just your pet, your family, and Dr. Bennett’s full attention in the place your pet feels safest.

What a House Call Looks Like

Visits are shaped to your family, not to a schedule. Dr. Bennett begins by sitting with you and your pet, listening carefully, and taking the time to understand what’s going on before recommending a plan. Whether it’s a routine wellness check, an illness work-up, or chronic disease management, every visit is unhurried and personal.

Services for Oakland Hills Pets

Dr. Bennett provides comprehensive in-home care for Oakland Hills families: wellness exams, vaccinations, dermatology, illness diagnostics, chronic disease management, hospice support, and more. See all veterinary services →

End-of-Life Care

When the time comes to say goodbye, Dr. Bennett’s sister practice Pets for Angels provides compassionate in-home pet euthanasia throughout the Oakland Hills and the East Bay.
