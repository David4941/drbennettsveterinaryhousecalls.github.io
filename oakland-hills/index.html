Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in the Oakland Hills

From Montclair’s village shops to the wooded hillside homes above, the Oakland Hills are a beloved community with deep roots and a strong sense of place. Whether you live in Montclair, Crocker Highlands, or one of the upper Piedmont-adjacent neighborhoods, Dr. Bennett brings calm, in-home veterinary care to Oakland Hills families and their pets.

In-home euthanasia means your pet does not need to travel, you do not need to leave, and the process can be shaped to your family rather than a clinic appointment.

The Visit, Step by Step

Visits are shaped to your family, not to a schedule. Dr. Bennett begins by sitting with you and your pet. Sedation comes first; the final step comes only after your pet is completely relaxed. The full process — including aftercare — is described on the in-home pet euthanasia page.
