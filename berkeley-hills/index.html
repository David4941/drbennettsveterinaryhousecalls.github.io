Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in the Berkeley Hills

From the homes along Grizzly Peak to the streets near Tilden Park, the Berkeley Hills are a uniquely beautiful corner of the East Bay — eucalyptus-lined ridges, sweeping bay views, and a tight-knit community. Dr. Bennett brings house-call veterinary care to Berkeley Hills families and their pets, including dignified in-home euthanasia.

The choice to euthanize at home means no carrier, no clinic anxiety, no waiting room — just your pet, your family, and the time you need to say goodbye.

What a Home Visit Looks Like

Visits are gentle and unhurried. Dr. Bennett sits with you and your pet first, listens, and only proceeds when you are ready. The in-home pet euthanasia page walks through the full process — sedation, the final step, and the time afterward.

Routine Care for Berkeley Hills Pets

In-home wellness visits are the everyday side of Dr. Bennett's practice. Berkeley Hills pets get exams, vaccines, skin and ear consults, illness work-ups, and hospice support — without ever leaving home. See all veterinary services →
