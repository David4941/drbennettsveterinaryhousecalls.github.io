A Peaceful Goodbye, in the Comfort of Home

When the time comes to say goodbye, no pet should spend their final moments in a stressful, unfamiliar place. In-home pet euthanasia allows your beloved companion to pass peacefully on their own bed, surrounded by family, free from the anxiety of car rides and clinic waiting rooms.

Dr. Bennett provides quiet, gentle, dignified end-of-life care throughout the East Bay. There is no rush — we take whatever time you and your family need.

Why a Mobile Vet?

  • No vet hospital waiting rooms. Your pet stays calm in their own environment.
  • You stay with your pet through the entire visit.
  • Personalized, unhurried care. Dr. Bennett spends real time with each patient.
  • Saves you time. No driving, no carrier, no lobby.

About Dr. Bennett

Dr. David Bennett is a California-licensed veterinarian (UC Davis, DVM 1997) with extensive clinical and diagnostic experience. He has dedicated his practice to bringing compassionate, expert care to East Bay families and their pets — at home, where they’re most comfortable.
