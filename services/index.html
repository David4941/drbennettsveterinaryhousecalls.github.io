Wellness & Medical Care

Routine and specialized house-call veterinary care — without the carrier, without the waiting room.

Call (925) 519-2316   Send a Message

For pets who find clinic visits stressful — and for families who prefer a calmer, more personal experience — Dr. Bennett brings the full range of general veterinary care directly to your home.

Pet Wellness

Comprehensive physical exams, vaccinations, parasite control, and microchipping — performed in your living room, on your pet’s favorite chair, with no fear or fuss.

Health Certificates

Health certificates for domestic animals, including those required for travel. Please call ahead to discuss the specific certificate you need so we can plan timing and paperwork.

Veterinary Dermatology

Skin problems and itching — sometimes the most maddening conditions for both pets and owners. Dr. Bennett offers both immediate relief and long-term diagnostic and treatment plans for chronic skin conditions.

Minor Injury Care

Abscesses, foxtails (a particular East Bay menace in spring and summer), lacerations, and other minor trauma can often be handled right in your home.

Illness Management

Vomiting, diarrhea, weight loss, anorexia, lethargy — when something is wrong but you’re not sure what, Dr. Bennett brings the diagnostic experience to figure it out and the calm of home to make it easier on your pet.

Chronic Disease Care

Long-term management of chronic conditions including diabetes, thyroid disease, kidney dysfunction, and others. Regular at-home visits make ongoing care less disruptive for both pet and family.

Hospice and Comfort Care

For pets in their final months, Dr. Bennett offers hospice services including subcutaneous fluid support, pain control, and nutritional support — helping keep your pet comfortable for as long as their quality of life allows.

In-Home Pet Euthanasia

When the time comes, a peaceful and dignified goodbye in the comfort of home. Read more about in-home pet euthanasia →

Specialist Referrals

When advanced diagnostics or surgery are needed, Dr. Bennett works with trusted partners across the Bay Area:

  • UC Davis Small Animal Teaching Hospital
  • East Bay Veterinary Specialists
  • Contra Costa Veterinary Emergency
  • Sage Veterinary Surgical Associates
