Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Concord

From the historic Todos Santos Plaza downtown to the foothill neighborhoods looking up at Mt. Diablo, Concord families have welcomed Dr. Bennett into their homes for many years. House-call veterinary care — and dignified in-home pet euthanasia — keep your pet calm in the place they know best.

For families facing the difficult decision to say goodbye to a beloved pet, in-home euthanasia means no carrier, no clinic waiting room, and no hurry. Your pet stays calm; you stay close; the time is yours.

What to Expect

Dr. Bennett begins each visit with a quiet conversation. There is no rush. Your pet receives gentle sedation first, drifting peacefully to sleep before anything else. Once your pet is fully relaxed, the final step is quick and painless. Afterward, you may stay with your pet as long as you wish.

For a complete description of the process, please see our in-home pet euthanasia page.
