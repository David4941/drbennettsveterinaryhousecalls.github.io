Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Concord

From the historic Todos Santos Plaza downtown to the foothill neighborhoods looking up at Mt. Diablo, Concord families have welcomed Dr. Bennett into their homes for many years. House-call veterinary care — and dignified in-home pet euthanasia — keep your pet calm in the place they know best.

When the moment to say goodbye is near, in-home euthanasia means no carrier, no clinic waiting room, and no hurry. Your pet stays calm; you stay close; the time is yours.

How Dr. Bennett Approaches a Visit

Each visit begins with conversation, never instructions. Your pet receives gentle sedation; the final step comes only once they are at peace. You stay with them as long as you wish. The in-home pet euthanasia page describes the full process in detail.

Concord Wellness and Medical Care

Routine veterinary care also comes to your door in Concord: wellness exams, vaccinations, skin and ear consults, chronic disease management, and hospice. The services page has the full list.
