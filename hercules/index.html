House-Call Veterinary Care in Hercules

From the Refugio Valley Park area, the planned neighborhoods of Victoria by the Bay, and the family streets near Pinole Valley Road, Dr. Bennett brings compassionate house-call veterinary care to Hercules families and their pets. No carriers, no waiting rooms, no unfamiliar smells — just calm, unhurried attention for your pet in the place they feel safest.

  • Wellness exams and vaccinations
  • Veterinary dermatology and skin care
  • Illness diagnostics and treatment
  • Chronic disease management — diabetes, thyroid, kidney
  • Hospice and comfort care
  • Health certificates

Why a House Call?

Many pets find clinic visits stressful — the car ride, the carrier, the unfamiliar smells, the other animals. For Hercules families who would rather their pet stay calm and comfortable, a house call means Dr. Bennett comes to you. There is no waiting room, no rush, and you stay with your pet through the entire visit.

End-of-Life Care

For families facing the decision of saying goodbye to a beloved pet, Dr. Bennett offers compassionate in-home euthanasia through Pets for Angels — a separate practice dedicated exclusively to peaceful, dignified end-of-life care at home.
