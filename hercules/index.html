Compassionate End-of-Life Care, at Home in Hercules

Hercules sits along the shore of San Pablo Bay, a community shaped by its history and refreshed by its modern neighborhoods. Whether you live in the historic downtown or one of the newer hillside developments, Dr. Bennett brings expert in-home veterinary care to Hercules families and their pets.

Choosing in-home euthanasia means your pet stays in surroundings they know, you stay close throughout, and the timing belongs to your family rather than a clinic schedule.

How a Visit Unfolds

Each visit begins with a quiet conversation. There is no agenda and no clock; you set the pace. Dr. Bennett walks through every step before anything begins, and only proceeds when you are ready. For the complete description of the process, please see our in-home pet euthanasia page.
